Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2025

Overview

Cowboy Quick Shot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Names or contact information
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Network information

Local Data Only

All game data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your best reaction times and performance statistics
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Achievement progress

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

Motion Detection

The app uses CoreMotion to detect when you flip your phone during gameplay. This motion data:

  • Is processed entirely on your device
  • Is never recorded or stored
  • Is never transmitted to our servers
  • Is used solely for game mechanics

Multiplayer Features

When using wireless multiplayer features:

  • Connection is peer-to-peer between devices
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • Only game state information is shared between connected devices
  • No personal information is exchanged

No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Third-party tracking tools

No Internet Connection Required

The app functions completely offline and does not require an internet connection to play.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our app.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, as we do not collect personal information from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact:

support@cowboyquickshot.com

By using Cowboy Quick Shot, you agree to this privacy policy.
