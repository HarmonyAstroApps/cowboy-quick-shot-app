Fast Photo Editing on iPhone

Short answer: Master brightness, contrast, and saturation. These 3 edits fix 90% of phone photos. Everything else is optional.

The Essential 3

Don't get lost in 50 edit options. Focus on these three:

  • Brightness — Is it too dark or blown out?
  • Contrast — Do subjects pop from background?
  • Saturation — Are colors vibrant or dull?

Your Edit Workflow

  1. Brightness first (it affects everything)
  2. Contrast second (to make subjects stand out)
  3. Saturation last (to make colors pop)
  4. Save and move on

Why This Works

iPhone cameras capture good data. Usually the image just needs brightness/contrast/saturation tweaked. Advanced edits are rarely needed for social sharing.
